Heritage Bake Shoppe began with the dream to positively impact the community, the dream to provide job opportunities, and the dream to create homestyle baked goods on demand. In September of 2020, Jason Miller's opened the doors of Heritage Bake Shoppe. They offered a line up of deli meats and cheeses, sandwiches made to order, fresh baked goods, coffees, grill seasonings and some bulk foods. When plans changed for them and they moved elsewhere, I, Tina took on the dream.

Here you can enjoy the nostalgic aroma of freshly baked bread, order a sandwich, custom made with fresh bread, sample high-quality meat and cheese, (imported from Amish country), and treat yourselves to exquisite danishes. Many of these recipes are ones we used at home growing up or have been perfected by other family and friends. We are blessed with an excellent team of experts who consistently create high-quality baked goods and sandwiches. We are proud to serve each of you and look forward to seeing you!
